The Caretaker now talks to you like a person, directly and in plain language:

- It always addresses you directly. It no longer prefixes messages with things like "@user:" or narrates how it's sending them -- every message is just what it would say to you, in everyday non-technical words.

- The first-night welcome is now only the welcome itself, with no "I was asked to output the following" preamble in front of it.

- On later nights the Caretaker opens with a short hello before it does anything -- e.g. "Hi, I'm the Caretaker for your Mind. Since you've said I can check for problems, I'm going to take a look now" -- worded from your saved preferences, so you always see it greet you and say what it's about to do before it gets to work. The behind-the-scenes trigger that starts the run is hidden from the chat, just like the welcome's is.
